All-inclusive Dining

7 Distinct Gourmet Best Restaurants in Cabo San Lucas
Savor a global gastronomic tour without leaving the comfort of the resort. Relish a different restaurant every night of the week, be it one of seven uniquely designed and themed gourmet best restaurants in Cabo San Lucas that deliver fresh, local ingredients with varied cuisines. Enjoy authentic Asian, Italian, or Mexican fare at Sen Lin, Lucca, or Frida, or Two-star Michelin chef Sidney Schutte will delight you with a 10-course tasting menu at Cocina de Autor.

Explore the flavors of Asia with a refined dinner at Loto, located in the exclusive Grand Velas Boutique Hotel and now available as a dining option for guests of Grand Velas Los Cabos. For seafood lovers, savor fine steaks and seafood at Velas 10 or enjoy local chocolate clams and ceviche paired with mezcal at Cabrilla. Whichever you choose, you can expect the finest ingredients, creative presentations, and warm hospitality.


Pair your meal with wine, mezcal, or tequila and choose from a variety of atmospheres to complete your dining experience.
